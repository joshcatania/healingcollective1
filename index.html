About

I am a trained Eye Movement Desensitizing and Reprocessing (EMDR) therapist. EMDR is a therapy used for treating trauma, anxiety and depression, focusing on changing how core memories are stored in the brain and body, reducing negative symptoms that are associated with negative memories by using eye movements.
